⚠️ Common Mistakes

Learn from common pitfalls and avoid mistakes that can cost your team valuable points.

Top Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overcomplicating designs: Simple, reliable solutions often outperform complex ones
  • Ignoring game rules: Always read the game manual thoroughly and check for updates
  • Poor time management: Plan your build season and stick to deadlines
  • Inadequate testing: Test early and often, especially autonomous routines
  • Neglecting documentation: Keep engineering notebooks and document your process
  • Not practicing driving: Driver skill is crucial - practice regularly
  • Forgetting safety: Follow all safety rules and use proper protective equipment
